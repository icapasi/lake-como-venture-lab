The phases

12 months of acceleration

From concept to first Proof of Concept, a structured journey in 4 phases with concrete milestones.

Phase 1 · Months 0-3

Technology Validation and Setup

Onboarding, intensive lab experimentation, proof-of-concept validation, and first scientific checkpoint.

Phase 2 · Months 3-6

Tech-Market Convergence

Market discovery with early adopters, demonstrable prototype development, initial tests with industrial partners, and value proposition definition.

Phase 3 · Months 6-9

Market Preparation and Scaling

Pilot projects with customers, unit economics validation, IP strategy consolidation, and regulatory compliance.

Phase 4 · Months 9-12

Investment Readiness and Demo Day

Pitch deck and data room finalization, financial projections, investor preparation with mock sessions, and final Demo Day.

The program

What the program includes

Everything you need to go from concept to first production run.

  • ✓ 12 months of housing within the Lecco campus
  • ✓ Scientific tutoring together with researchers from Politecnico di Milano
  • ✓ Lab access at Politecnico for prototyping and testing
  • ✓ Local enterprise network to validate product-market fit
  • ✓ Mentorship from successful founders and industrialization experts
  • ✓ Investor access and Business Angels network for growth capital

Not a generic accelerator. A program built for hardware deep tech teams that need labs, supply chain and industrial expertise to go from prototype to production.

The labs

The entire Politecnico di Milano lab network

Not just the Lecco campus: selected startups will have access to labs across the entire Politecnico system to develop and validate their prototypes.

From lab to product

Politecnico di Milano provides its entire network of research laboratories and experimental infrastructure to support each startup from prototyping to technical validation.

  • Access to labs at the Lecco campus and across the entire Politecnico network
  • Advanced instrumentation for testing, materials characterization, and prototyping
  • Scientific mentorship from specialized researchers and researchers
  • Support in proof-of-concept validation and pre-industrial development
